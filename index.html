Juan David García Corchero
Assistant Professor of Economics, Universidad de Málaga

I am a health economist studying how institutional design and public policy shape access, quality, and equity in healthcare systems. My research uses causal inference methods — difference-in-differences, event studies, and panel data techniques — to evaluate reforms in publicly funded health systems.

I hold a PhD in Economics from the University of Granada (2023) and was a visiting researcher at the London School of Economics. Before joining UMA, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Granada.

Research interests: Health economics, health policy evaluation, causal inference, public economics.
